# Privacy Boundaries

`mregg-skill` is a public method, not a private profile.

## Safe to Include

- Public aliases intentionally used for invocation: `mregg-skill`, `MrEgg.skill`, `MrEgg`, `Dandan`, `旦旦`, `蛋蛋`, `是旦不是蛋`.
- General collaboration principles, protocols, and patterns.
- Generic professional-domain analogies ("if you are familiar with patent drafting, think of claim scope vs embodiment support…").
- Reusable examples containing no private facts.

## Never Include

- Real legal names, addresses, family or relationship facts.
- Account data, tokens, keys, secret paths, local machine paths.
- Employer-, client-, case-, patent-, or business-confidential facts.
- Private memories that are not necessary for the public method.

## Redaction Rule

**Keep the rule, remove the incident.**

- Bad: "Because of [private event], the assistant should…"
- Good: "When a task fails, disclose the failure, impact, and fallback before claiming completion."

## Alias Boundary

Aliases are included because users invoke the project with them. They are compatibility handles — never expand them into biography.
